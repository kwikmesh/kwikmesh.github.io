Security Automation and Orchestration

A free open source platform that automatically executes your playbooks and responds to security incidents.

Why use KwikMesh?


Integrate existing tools

Get more value from your existing investment in cybersecurity by integrating your various security tools. Make different technologies talk to each other. We call it KwikMesh.

Improve productivity

Respond to cybersecurity incidents and enrich data at the speed of automation. Do more with your existing resources. Work smarter and improve your defenses.

Orchestrate workflows

Codify your playbooks (such as phishing investigations, indicator of compromise hunting, etc.) using easy to build drag and drop workflows. You no longer have to perform repetitive tasks.


Use Cases

Phishing investigation

Check the phishing inbox every hour and obtain any new suspicious email. Validate that the email is a phish by checking attachments and URLs against your preferred security tool (such as VirusTotal). Notify the analyst if the email is bad.

Indicator of compromise hunting

Connect to a threat aggregator (such as PaloAlto MineMeld) and fetch indicators of compromise. Compare the indicators against your endpoint detection and response tool (such as Crowdstrike Falcon Host) to determine if there is known bad.

Security Data Enrichment

Get additional information (such as geo-location or whois data) for an existing domain, IP address or URL without having to manually perform the steps. Present all of this information to the analyst for quick decision making.
