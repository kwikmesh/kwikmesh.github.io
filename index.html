Why use KwikMesh?

Integrate existing tools

Get more value from your existing investment in cybersecurity by integrating your various security tools. Make different technologies to talk to each other. We call it KwikMesh.

Improve productivity

Respond to cybersecurity incidents and enrich data at the speed of automation. Do more with your existing resources. Work smarter and improve your defenses.

Orchestrate workflows

Codify your playbooks (e.g. phishing investigations, indicator of compromise hunting, etc.) using easy to build drag and drop workflows. You no longer have to perform repetitive tasks.
